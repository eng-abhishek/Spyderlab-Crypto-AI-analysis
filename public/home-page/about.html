About SpyderLab

SpyderLab is the leading provider of public information through its dedicated APIs and huge database. It primarily aims to provide deep analytics data at par with the search query in real time. Users can search for publicly posted information on diverse platforms including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and WhatsApp through either phone number or email id. At SpyderLab, we strive to provide information scraping the darknet such as malicious blockchain transactions, cybercrime, criminal activities, drug sales, and other illicit activities. This information is specifically meant for law enforcement agencies to combat increasing cybercrimes. Thousands of sites across multiple darknets are added to our database every day, letting users analyze the data for specific use cases.
